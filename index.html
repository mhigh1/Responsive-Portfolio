About Me

Michael High is a general technologist with over 15 years of experience in the Information Technology industry. A graduate of the University of West Georgia with a bachelor’s degree in Business Administration (BBA) and a specialization in Management of Information Systems (MIS) he has experience in multiple technology disciplines including: Windows administration, messaging and call center technologies, DevOps, scripting (PowerShell), networking, storage, automation, virtualization, security, and operations management.

Michael currently works as a Senior Lifecycle Operations Engineer for GE Digital in the configuration and vulnerability management space.

When not working in the digital frontier, he enjoys unwinding with friends, exploring the city, or by playing video games. As an Eagle Scout (BSA) he’s a strong believer of giving back to the community and participates in volunteer efforts with organizations such as March of Dimes, Power My Learning, and most recently Junior Achievement of Georgia. Michael is a student of early American history and is working on an outdoor adventure curriculum with hopes to one day share his passion with others through Scouting.
